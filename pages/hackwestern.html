Our Journey

The Design Process

As all design projects start, our team met for the first time in April 2019 for a brainstorming session. Our initial goal was to come up with a cohesive theme. This year, an extra challenge was to come up with an underlying narrative that the participants would experience from the application process all the way to the event day.

We finally settled on a space theme that would incorporate Western’s purple interlaced with orange hues to create a futuristic space feel. Our team also developed the anchoring statement: Build Your Moonshot

From here, our team quickly began producing assets to be used throughout our branding. Aliens, planets, and astronauts were created and saved for future use. Our next large deliverable was our brand book and sponsorship package. This would serve as an anchor for all future designs and products.

For the rest of the summer, we worked on creating the physical assets and designs for the event. This included bulk purchasing RGB light strips from Ali-Express, cutting out planets and nametags, and plastering together makeshift alien and astronaut costumes.

When the time finally came, our design team was proud to finally unveil Hack Western 6 to the public, and welcome the 600+ individuals that came to attend!

Video Branding

I also had the pleasure of leading the video branding assets for the event. I produced several short videos to be used on social media platforms for promotional purposes using the branding that we created earlier. As the event drew near, I also created a video to be used during our opening ceremonies showcasing all the team members. This was a fun project that allowed me to try 3D compositing for the first time.
